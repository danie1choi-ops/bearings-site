The problem

The world is becoming harder to read.

Every day brings new headlines, new opinions, new crises and new distractions.

Most will not matter next year.

A small number will quietly reshape how we work, what things cost, where we live and what opportunities exist.

The difficult part is telling the difference.

The promise
Bearings does not try to make you follow more news. It helps you understand which developments are worth carrying forward.
